Starting As Soon As I Could

I Knew That I Lived To Overcome

Joining the military as a teen, I jumped at the chance to prove to myself I could make it through and fight on the worst battlefields on earth. It was right at the height of war and I went in as a infantryman and soon become a grenadier. With the World Trade Center images still burned into my brain from highschool I was ready to give all I had. I wanted to see how much I could handle, what I was really made of and be pushed to my absolute limit and beyond. Now I have no questions, I know.

Brotherhood Was Never A Choice

It Is A Way Of Life

I wanted to give my all and I did, I paid for all the sins I had committed in the past and then some. Through blood, sweat and tears I shed who I use to be, but found a family and a brotherhood. Now that I have left the service I want to be able to give back to those that kept me going in the darkest of times. They were there when I needed them, and now I want nothing more but to return the favor. Now that I have the chance, I'll be there for all the active duty and veterans who have been family.
